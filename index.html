    An entertainer looking to change the world

           Hello there, my name is Roman Delgado, or, as I am more well known as, dead (deaddelgado) online. I am an Internet Entertainer, I do this by Livestreaming and recording videos for entertainment and video-game education. Other than digital entertainment, I enjoy drawing in my free time, typically doing digital art such as graphic design. My entertainment field is fairly diverse in the realms of; Music, Video Games, Story Reading, Tutorials, and Food. I am a very positive person who always finds a way to look at the brighter side of any dark or rough situation that is presented or occurring in my life. Positivity is something I feel that is important to apply in our lives as much as possible and I try to spread this to those around me; entertaining others is a big hobby of mine! Any time I'm out with friends or even hanging out in a Livestream, I'm normally the first to suggest something to do, or come up with a topic to talk about, and give a random fun fact, I just tend to get others active and thinking. I love the horror genre with films, books, games, etc, so that may explain my theme, including my name. I'd really like to do more horror related content as my channel grows, but I also don't want to be known just for horror, we'll see what happen, and I hope you enjoy it every step of the way! Let me know if you have any suggestions or ideas that I could try out or mess with, I'm always open to them!
